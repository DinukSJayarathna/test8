About

Undergraduate Researcher-in-Training

I'm a BSc (Hons) Biotechnology student at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT), currently building a foundation across biochemistry, molecular biology, microbiology, genetics, and cell culture. I'm most drawn to the molecular and microbial side of biology — how cells work, how genetic mechanisms can be studied and manipulated, and what that reveals about biological systems more broadly.

Outside coursework, I explore the same instinct for close observation through macro photography — documenting the small-scale detail of plant and insect life that's easy to walk past.
